The experience
Step 01
Open & walk
Launch Dwell, enable AR mode, and hold up your phone. The city layers itself onto your view — events annotated in space, scaled by proximity.
Step 02
Tap to discover
Tap any pin for details: what's on, who's going, how far. One tap to save, one more to navigate. No feeds, no scroll, no algorithm.
Step 03
Add local knowledge
Know about a recurring open mike or a rooftop with a view? Add it. Verified residents become the editorial layer the map never had.
